# Intro to Stage Management

Discover the crucial role of the SM team, from attending rehearsals and managing props to calling the show.

Stage management is the backbone of any successful production, coordinating between all departments and ensuring everything runs smoothly from first rehearsal to final performance.

## What We'll Cover

- Role of the Stage Manager, Assistant Stage Manager, and Deputy Stage Manager

Perfect for detail-oriented people who enjoy bringing order to creative chaos!
