This is the perfect starting point for anyone interested in the technical side of theatre - whether you're a complete beginner or have some experience already.

## What We'll Cover

- **Theatre Tour**: Explore our technical spaces, from the workshop to the tech desk
- **Equipment Showcase**: See our professional-grade lighting, sound, and set construction equipment
- **Introductions**: Meet the backstage committee members who'll be leading future sessions
- **Q&A Session**: Get answers to all your questions about technical theatre at the NNT
- **Social Time**: Chat with other newcomers and existing members over refreshments

## What to Expect

This is a relaxed, informal session designed to make you feel welcome. No prior experience is needed - just bring your curiosity and enthusiasm!

We'll show off some of the cool tech we have, explain how our technical teams work, and help you understand what opportunities are available throughout the year.

## Perfect For

- Complete beginners who want to learn about technical theatre
- Students with some experience looking to get involved backstage at the NNT
- Anyone curious about what happens behind the scenes

Come as you are - we're excited to meet you!
