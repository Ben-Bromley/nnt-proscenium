## Safety Requirements

### What You Must Wear
- Closed-toe shoes (steel-toed preferred)
- Long trousers (no shorts)
- Eye protection when using power tools
- Hearing protection for loud equipment

### What to Avoid
- Loose clothing that could catch in machinery
- Long hair must be tied back
- No jewelry that could get caught
- No open-toed shoes or sandals
