## Set and Scenery

### Scenic Elements
- No safety curtain or house tabs in any space
- 2x black drapes with clips (4 x 3m)
- 8m tab track in 3 sections with runners
- 8x4m gauze with ties to the top
- 8x5m white cyc with eyelets to the top
- Various 4ft wide flats at 8 and 10ft height

### Staging
- **19x Prolyte StageDex 2 x 1m**
- **3x Prolyte StageDex 1 x 1m**
- Legs available at 20, 40, 60, and 80cm

### Fire Safety
- All scenery, drapes, and backdrops must be fire resistant
- We may carry out controlled flame tests and disallow materials that are not suitably flame retardant

### Electrical Safety
- All electrical equipment must be in good condition with current PAT certificate
- Must have appropriate protective circuits and be wired in accordance with current IEE regulations
- All equipment and staff must comply with the Electricity at Work Regulations 1989
- Electrical or rigging equipment may be disallowed if deemed dangerous or inappropriate -->
