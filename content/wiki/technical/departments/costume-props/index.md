## Overview

Costume, props, and make-up are integral parts of any show. These creative and rewarding roles let you work on the design and execution of three key visual areas, collaborating with the production team to make the show look as good as it can on stage.

**No experience necessary!** Any training you need will be provided, and there's always support from experienced members and the Costume, Props and Make-Up Manager.
