# Technical Theatre

Welcome to the technical side of the Nottingham New Theatre! Our technical department handles all the behind-the-scenes magic that brings productions to life.

## Overview

With professional-grade equipment and a supportive learning environment, the NNT's technical department offers opportunities for everyone from complete beginners to experienced technicians.

## What We Do

- **Lighting Design**: Create atmospheric and dramatic lighting for all productions
- **Sound Design**: From subtle underscores to complex soundscapes
- **Video Design**: Projection mapping and visual effects
- **Technical Direction**: Coordinating all technical aspects of shows
- **Rigging and Setup**: Installing equipment safely and efficiently
