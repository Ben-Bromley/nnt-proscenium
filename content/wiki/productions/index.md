# The Production Process

<!-- The "show bible," a step-by-step guide from idea to closing night. -->

Whether you're interested in performing, directing, or working behind the scenes, there are many ways to get involved with productions at the Nottingham New Theatre.

## Getting Started

The best way to get involved is to:

1. **Subscribe to our [mailing list](/mailing-list/)** to hear about opportunities
2. **Come to Season Launch** events to meet creative teams
3. **Attend auditions** - open to everyone, no experience required
4. **Volunteer during get-ins** to gain hands-on experience
